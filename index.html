So... What is this all about?

This website was made for my personal planning needs, but can also be used by others;
I desgined it based on the principals persented in "Atomic Habits" by James Clear:

  • Habit Stacking: I will do ___ at ___ during ____, then I will do ___ etc
  • Giving high priority to the NOT-to-do list
  • Strucutring micro and macro plans and creating your own deadlines to stay on track with values
  • To make sure you are sticking with your values or the values you want, you will use an Esienhower square
  • Included parameters for fault tolerance, and timing how long tasks take
  • Graph energy levels throughout the day, and plan deepwork based on those
  • A system to break larger tasks into smaller ones (side quests)
  • Checkboxes, and other dopeamine producing systems spread throughout the website
  • And many other smaller principals applied throughout
